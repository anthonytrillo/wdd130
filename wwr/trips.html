Grand Canyon - Extreme Adventure

Immerse yourself in the most exciting adventure of your life in the Grand Canyon rapids. This trip is designed for adrenaline lovers and offers level 5 rapids that will test your skills.

During this journey you will experience:

  • 8 hours of intense rafting in white waters
  • Spectacular views of unique geological formations
  • Expert guides with more than 10 years of experience
  • Gourmet lunch on the riverbank

Recommended level: Experienced

Colorado River - Family Adventure

Enjoy a perfect family day navigating through the calm and occasionally exciting waters of the Colorado River. This trip is perfectly balanced to offer fun and safety for all ages.

This excursion includes:

  • 5 hours of rafting in moderately difficult waters (level 2-3)
  • Stops for swimming in crystal clear waters
  • Guides specialized in family groups
  • Family picnic included

Recommended level: Beginner to Intermediate - Perfect for children 8 years and older!

3-Day Expedition - Wild River

Experience the complete adventure with our three-day expedition through the majestic Wild River. This trip will allow you to completely disconnect from routine and immerse yourself in nature.

The expedition includes:

  • 3 days of adventure with 2 nights camping under the stars
  • Gourmet meals prepared by our specialized chef
  • Rapids of various levels (2-4) for a varied experience
  • Complete high-quality camping equipment
  • Complementary activities such as hiking and wildlife observation

Recommended level: Intermediate
